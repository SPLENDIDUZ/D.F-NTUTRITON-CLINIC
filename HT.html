What is diabetes?

Diabetes is a metabolic disorder, raising either due to;

  1. Relative or absolute deficiency of a digestive hormone called “Insulin”
  2. Inability or resistance of body-cells to use metabolism of dietary carbohydrates, fats and proteins into disarry.

Types of diabetes

  • Insulin Dependent Diabetes Mellitus (IDDM) or Juvenile ( Diabetes type 1)
  • . Non-Insulin-Dependent Diabetes Mellitus (NIDDM) or Maturity onset (Diabetes type 2)





CAUSES OF DIABETES

  • Hereditary
  • Inadequate physical work
  • Incorrect Dietary habits
  • Effects of certain hormones
  • Side-effects of certain drugs
  • Other illnesses
  • Psychological factors

SYMPTOMS OF DIABETES

  • Polyurea (excessive and frequent urination)
  • Polydipsia (dryness of mouth and excessive thirst)
  • Polyphagia (excessive hunger)
  • Loss of weight
  • Weakness, fatigue and body-ache
  • Mental fatigue and lack of concentration
  • Wound-infection and delayed healing
